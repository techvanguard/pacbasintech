As systems for the connecting devices and the internet of things become more ubiquitous, we've kept up with the need for more interactive analytics. By first researching a specific use case, we simulated the bundle of sensors that are at one's disposal (e.g. Anaren Atmosphere prototyping on a micro controller board) in a type mobile device to capture data on the individual's movements. After ingesting it through a standard architecture (e.g Kinesis & DynamoDB) and conducting some interactive analytics on such data we realized that it's possible to match the personalization from the data to the individual. Such an approach can be easily extended to a new user case from industrial IOT for equipment failure to medtech wearables for health. With IOT prototype devices, once the data is streamed into the client's architecture we can work on the full gamut of analytics to get more meaningful insights.
